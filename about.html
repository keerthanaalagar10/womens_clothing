Our Story

Founded in 2018 by fashion enthusiast Priya Sharma, Elegance was born out of a passion for creating stylish, comfortable clothing that empowers women to express their unique personalities.

What started as a small boutique in Bangalore has now grown into a beloved brand with a nationwide presence, offering a curated collection of women's clothing and accessories that blend contemporary trends with timeless elegance.

Our journey has been guided by a simple philosophy: fashion should be accessible, sustainable, and make you feel confident. Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed with attention to detail, quality fabrics, and ethical production practices.

Priya Sharma

Founder & Creative Director
