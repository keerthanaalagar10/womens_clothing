Frequently Asked Questions

We offer standard shipping (4-6 business days) for ₹49 and express shipping (1-3 business days) for ₹149. Free shipping is available on orders above ₹1,999. International shipping is currently available to select countries.

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Please note that certain items like jewelry and sale items may be final sale. Return shipping is free for exchanges.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. If you have any issues with tracking, please contact our customer support team.
